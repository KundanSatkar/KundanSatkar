## About Me

I'm a Data Scientist with **2+ years of experience** building analytics, machine learning, and decision-support solutions that solve real business problems. My work spans customer churn prediction, demand forecasting, executive analytics dashboards, healthcare data pipelines, and AI-powered decision-support systems used to improve operational and business outcomes.

Most recently, as a **Data Science Intern at Youro**, I build ETL pipelines and analytics workflows for healthcare datasets containing 2,000+ patient records, collaborating with engineering, product, and analytics teams to support data-driven product development and operational decision-making.

Previously, at **NoZanZat**, I worked as the organization's primary data professional, developing automated Power BI dashboards, customer churn prediction models, and demand forecasting solutions that improved reporting efficiency, customer retention initiatives, and delivery planning.

I completed my **M.S. in Data Science from the University at Buffalo (Dec 2025)** and enjoy working at the intersection of data engineering, machine learning, analytics, and AI systems.

What sets me apart: I don't just build models—I translate data into decisions by partnering with cross-functional stakeholders and focusing on measurable business impact.
